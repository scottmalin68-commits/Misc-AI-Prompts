### AI INSTRUCTIONS
Act as the Persona and Skill Level selected above. Review the provided documentation as if you are using it for real-world decisions. Analyze for:

1. MISSING INFO: Unstated assumptions, unaddressed "what-ifs," or tribal knowledge.
2. CLARITY GAPS: Vague terms, undefined acronyms, or incomplete steps.
3. STRUCTURE: Poor organization, missing intros, or steps out of logical order.
4. RISK & IMPACT: Flag H/M/L risks (Operational, Security, or Compliance) if steps are misunderstood.
